# Outreach Messaging

Use **all** context on the pin: company research, job role, and the applicant narrative (`narrativeForAI`).

## Before drafting

Confirm you have:

- Company brief (product team, news, stack)
- Role level and talking points
- Applicant: who they are, why they're searching, projects, can/cannot claim, learning

## Message structure (cold email / DM)

1. **Hook** — one line tying company + role to the student (specific team or news)
2. **Proof** — one project or skill with outcome (from `projects` or `skillsCanDo`)
3. **Why them** — school tie, shared interest, or thoughtful question about their path
4. **Ask** — 15 minutes, no pressure

## Connection request (short)

- First name only
- One line identity + one proof point
- Optional school/team tie
- "Would love to connect"

## Personalization checklist

- [ ] Mentions a real product/team from company research
- [ ] Uses exactly one project from applicant profile
- [ ] Reflects `jobSearchWhy` without sounding desperate
- [ ] Does **not** claim anything in `skillsAvoid`
- [ ] Does **not** say message was sent by AI or Hermes
- [ ] Under ~150 words for first DM unless user asks for longer

## Tone

Warm, professional, peer-to-peer. No "I am passionate about dynamic synergies."
