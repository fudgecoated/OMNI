# Weave Deck Asset Pack

This folder gathers the material needed to build a polished hackathon slide deck for Weave. It is a planning and source pack only: no screenshots have been captured yet, and no app behavior changes are implied by these docs.

## Deck Positioning

Weave starts with job search because that is where the pain is sharpest for us, but the bigger product is about useful connections:

> Weave helps people find and follow through on the conversations that can change their trajectory.

Use the job-search flow as the concrete demo wedge. Use the larger connection story for vision, impact, and memorability.

## Strongest Hooks

- "The anxiety is loud. The upside is invisible."
- "Cold outreach increases the surface area of luck."
- "The best opportunities are often not posted. They are passed."
- "Your future is partly hidden inside conversations with people you have not met yet."
- "Close friends give you support. Weak ties give you reach."

## Recommended Deliverables

Create three deck artifacts from this pack:

1. **Main judging deck:** emotional, visual, 5 slides, demo-first.
2. **Technical appendix:** architecture, AI/tool use, data flow, demo reliability, and feature status.
3. **Screenshot set:** 5 images for submission visual review, plus optional extras for the deck.
