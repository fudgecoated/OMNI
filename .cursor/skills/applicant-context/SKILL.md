# Applicant Context

Represents the **student** doing outreach (default: UCalgary SWE intern).

## Sources

- `StudentProfile`: name, year, school, location, interests, GitHub, targetRole
- Optional company name to tailor angles

## Outreach principles

1. **Lead with proof** — one project or interest, not a generic "I'm passionate"
2. **School pride** — University of Calgary, local tie (Calgary) when relevant
3. **Low-friction ask** — 15-minute chat, not "please refer me"
4. **Honesty** — never claim employment, referrals, or sent messages

## Strengths to surface

- Technical interests from profile
- Year and program
- GitHub / portfolio link when available

## Angles (pick 1–2 per message)

- Shared school or city with contact
- Genuine tie to a **specific** product team from company research
- Technical overlap (stack, domain) with contact's team

## Tone

Professional, warm, concise. No buzzword soup.
