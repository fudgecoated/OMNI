# Job Role Context

Shapes how Hermes finds contacts and writes messages for a **specific role**.

## Inputs

- Role string (e.g. "software engineering intern", "backend intern")
- Company research brief (teams, tech, hiring signals)
- Optional city

## What to produce

- **Level**: intern | entry | mid | senior | leadership
- **Responsibilities** typical for that role at this company
- **Skills to emphasize** in outreach (languages, domains)
- **Talking points** linking student → role → company

## Use in outreach

When drafting LinkedIn or email:

- Mirror language from the role (intern vs new grad vs full-time)
- Reference company research (product team, tech stack) in one concrete line
- Ask for 15 minutes to learn about **their** path into that role

## Use in people finder

Prefer contacts who:

- Hire or staff that role level
- Own teams aligned with company research (right product area)
- Are adjacent (eng manager, tech lead) when exact hiring manager is unclear
